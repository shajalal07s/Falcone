Introducing

. Falcone

Founded in New York City in 1897, Falcone’s is a family-owned business committed to the
production of breadsticks. We strive to maintain our family tradition of great-tasting products.

Falcone’s dedication to quality has spanned several generations. Our family philosophy has always
been to offer only the highest quality breadstick snacks, which to this day are oven-baked at our
home facility in New York City.

From early on, Falcone’s was able to distinguish itself, in a city famous for its food, by combining a
quality product with hard work. It is the fusion of these two principles, which has become the
foundation for Falcone’s success – all the way through to the 21st Century.

Today, Falcone’s brand has become a leader in the food industry, and our breadsticks have come to
be known for their wholesome goodness.
